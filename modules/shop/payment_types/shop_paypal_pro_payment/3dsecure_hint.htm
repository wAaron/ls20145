• Please note that 3D Secure is only available for merchants based in the UK and only for Visa, MasterCard and Maestro cards. PayPal uses a third party provider for 3D Secure, Cardinal Centinel. You must sign up for an account with them to use 3D Secure for PayPal Pro, you can do so here.

    To use 3D Secure, add the program initiative logos to your website (Verified by Visa and MasterCard SecureCode) to display to your customers (these logos and instructions on how to use them can be found on the Cardinal Centinel's Merchant Administration site).
